Introduction:

I am a PhD student at University of Gothenburg, Sweden under the supervision of Prof. Aarne Ranta My main research interests are in the area of natural language modeling and computational resources development. Currently, I am working in the Grammatical Framework group in University of Gothenburg, Sweden. As a part of my PhD studies, I have developed computational resources (both syntactical and lexical) for six Indo-Iranian languages including Hindi, Urdu, Persian, Punjabi, Nepali, and Sindhi. These resources are in the form of software libraries and encode different syntactical and lexical aspects of these natural languages. Historically, such resources have proved to be very useful in building domain specific application grammars both commercial and academic. Further, we are exploring the possibilities of using these resources to build a multilingual arbitrary text translator.
